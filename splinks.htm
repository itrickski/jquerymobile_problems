GPA/Hours Requirements for Aid

In order to retain federal financial aid, you must make Satisfactory Academic Progress.

Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP) is measured by your GPA, the percentage of credit hours earned in relation to attempted hours, and the requirements of your academic program. Remember that SAP is also required to continue receiving the Hope Scholarship.

To maintain SAP, you must earn a satisfactory grade in at least 67% of your total attempted credit hours (TEST SAME PAGE LINK ON A NEW PAGE SAP Calculation Chart).
